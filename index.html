Why isn't this on the App Store?

I would have preferred to publish the app on the app store, but app sandboxing on OSX makes it impossible to read arbitrary files not created by your app.

Files like the mod manifest file that lists all the mods.
And individual mod files.
Which breaks the whole purpose of the app.
